Hiking

Exploring Queensland one hike at a time

Queensland has beautiful landscapes and plenty to explore. My favorite way of doing so is by different trail hikes. From waterfalls to mountain tops and bays, there is always a way to explore this beautiful landscape on foot. Hiking in Queensland requires general fitness and a good set of hiking boots. Safety is very important on these hikes and it is iof great importance to have all the essentials with you. Always have a back-up supply of food and water in case of unexpected delays. Make sure to also carry first aid essentials with you. Happy hiking guys!
